About Me

Chenemi Zekeri is a budding front-end developer, medical student and spoken-word poet. She is currently participating in the Start.ng training program. She is passionate about community health and development, and is an advocate for SDGs 3, 4 and 5 (Good Health and Well-being, Quality Education and Gender Equality). She spends most of her free time either working on projects related to these interests, reading a book or getting lost in her own mind.
